About Us

Our Workshop was founded in the year 2004, and have been located at Vinayaka Nagar Main St, Rukmini Nagar, Bengaluru, Karnataka-73 for the last 15 years as an auto repair center and collision repair shop. Since the beginning, our business has been focused on building strong customer relationships and providing the highest quality work on each and every automotive repair. Our technicians stay current with the latest tools and changing technology through training so we can service all cars, new and old. The right equipment and knowledge makes the difference. We are equipped with the latest and most sophisticated tools to perform all types of repairs and necessary diagnostics for today’s computer controlled modern automobiles. We provide mechanical and accident repairs. Our shop will work with your insurance company and lends assistance on claims. V K Motors will also work with your after-market warranties company as well as insurance companies.
